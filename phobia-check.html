Phobias Check

Answer the following questions to assess your phobias.

1. Do you feel extremely anxious when you encounter certain objects or situations (e.g., spiders, heights, etc.)?

2. Do you try to avoid situations or places that trigger your fear?

3. When faced with a phobic situation, do you experience rapid heartbeat, sweating, or dizziness?

4. Do you feel a strong desire to flee or escape when confronted by your fear?

5. Has your fear interfered with your daily life or relationships?
